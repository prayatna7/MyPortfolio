🐅 BaghChal Rules

Game Setup

  • The board consists of 25 connected points.
  • Four tigers start at the four corners.
  • Twenty goats enter the board one by one.

Tiger Rules

  • Tigers move along connected lines.
  • A tiger can jump over a goat to capture it.
  • The captured goat is removed.
  • Tigers win by capturing enough goats.

Goat Rules

  • Goats are placed one at a time.
  • During placement, goats cannot move.
  • After all goats enter, they can move one step.
  • Goats cannot capture tigers.

Winning Conditions

  • Tigers win by capturing all 20 goats.
  • Goats win by trapping all tigers.\nLeaving no legal moves for all four tigers.

Strategy

The tiger must attack wisely while goats must work together. A single mistake can change the entire game.

About Baghchal Game

Baghchal is a traditional Nepali strategy board game played between four tigers and twenty goats. The tigers try to capture goats while the goats work together to surround and trap the tigers.

This online version of Baghchal recreates the classic board game experience with an interactive interface built using modern web technologies.

How To Play Baghchal

1. Tiger Movement

Tigers move across connected points on the board and can capture goats by jumping over them.

2. Goat Placement

Goats are placed one by one on empty positions of the board.

3. Winning

Tigers win by capturing enough goats, while goats win by trapping all tigers.

Baghchal Game Rules

  • There are four tigers and twenty goats.
  • The tiger player moves first.
  • Tigers capture goats by jumping over them.
  • Goats cannot capture tigers.
  • The goats win when all tigers become unable to move.
  • The tigers win by capturing enough goats.
